### Controls
#### Keyboard And Mouse
- WASD To Move
- Mouse To Look
- Left Click To Fire
- Space To Jump
#### Gamepad
- Left Stick To Move
- Right Stick To Look
- Right Trigger To Fire
- Bottem Button To Jump
